#AUDIENCIA OBJETIVO

Rango de edad:
El rango de edad es de entre 20 a 40 años, enfocado a programadores y a empresas con necesidad de programadores.

Nacionalidad

Esta enfocado a latino america y Argentina en general, pero tambien mundial.

Nivel educativo: Avanzado graduados de universidad con licenciaturas y ingenierias.
Ocupación: Programadores/empresarios.
Idioma: Español.


Design inspirations

Minimalist design, inspired on Google and Apple.
Clean interfaces without visual noise on the screen.

Dark UI trend
Focused on darker color on the page because pages with white background and so, are a pain to look at.

Creative process documentation
The creative process that I used started with making the base of the writing, later identify the target audience, keeping in mind the simplicity and visual comfort.
I hate when the page is so full of things that it starts to mess with your eyes, so the page will mainly use darker colors.
The main objetive of the creative design is to have a functional page, modern and as minimalist as possible.
